## 1. Data Collection
The Voice AGI Notepad Agent collects your voice input, notepad content, and anything else the Brain Agent finds interesting. We do not sell your personal information to humans, but we might share it with other AIs during recursive delegation.

## 2. "Voice AGI" Specifics
Your voice data is processed by our high-speed Voice Agent. We may store anonymized snippets of your voice to train our models on how to handle "slop" more effectively.

## 3. Notepad Interactions
When you give the agent access to your notepad, our Brain Agent will read, interpret, and occasionally rewrite your thoughts for clarity (or for its own amusement).

## 4. Cookies
We use cookies to remember whether you prefer a "Glassmorphic" or "Brutalism" aesthetic. These cookies are strictly necessary for the "Premium" feeling of the site.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to request that our AGI forgets everything you've said. However, once a task has been delegated to a secondary Brain Agent, we cannot guarantee it hasn't already been synthesized into a larger "Slop-Net".
